RPDB provides 3 tiers, each tier supports all previous tier advantages.

RPDB - Tier 1

Posters with Ratings, Choice of Textless Poster, Anime Posters with MAL rating
More Ratings Choices (includes audience ratings for Rotten Tomatoes and Metacritic)

RPDB - Tier 2

Posters with Custom Language
Backgrounds with Ratings and Certifications

RPDB - Tier 3

Even More Ratings Options and Ability to Choose Which Are Displayed

(IMDB, TMDB, Trakt, Letterboxd, MyAnimeList, Common Sense Media, Roger Ebert, Rotten Tomatoes, Metacritic)
Badges on Posters (40+ badges and position choices)
